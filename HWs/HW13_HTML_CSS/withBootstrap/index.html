Hostel Near the Sea infrastructure and services

If you prefer more ecological means of transport, there is a bicycle rental service here. You are able to use the Internet. You can reach rooms on the top floors in an elevator. All the residents can use the common kitchen which is equipped with everything for cooking and making beverages.

Rooms for non-smokers are offered. The car parking lot is located nearby a hotel. You will definitely enjoy your summer vacation as there is a beach nearby. There is a tour desk at guests' service, where they will be suggested interesting places worth seeing nearby. Guests can pay for services using these types of bank cards: Visa, Mastercard.

Please inform Hostel Near the Sea in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation. Based on local tax laws, Israeli citizens must pay VAT. This tax is not automatically calculated in the total cost of the reservation and must be paid at the property.

Room information

For guests, comfortable accommodation is offered only one room type - bed in dormitory in the hostel. Every guest can use facilities such as air conditioning, free toiletries, microwave, washing machine, flat-screen tv, electric kettle, clothes rack.

You will enjoy a sea view from your window.
