Where to watch movies online? A movie evening always starts with a very time-consuming and frustrating search for where to watch movies online. JustWatch is there to help you find all the movies you can stream legally in the UK and make your life easier.

Below you find the list of movies available to stream online via Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Now TV and 24 other providers, organized by popularity. If you have one or more flat-rate subscriptions, use the WatchBar to filter the movies available online on services with monthly subscription like Netflix, Now TV and Amazon Prime Video. You are searching for movies to rent or for movies to buy? Use our « Price » filter or check out the most popular providers for buying and renting movies: Google Play Movies, iTunes and Amazon Video. Our « Price » filter is also very useful to find free movies online. Having kids? Kids movies can be easily found on Netflix Kids dedicated page or by using our age-rating filter. You can also view all of the upcoming movies and TV series coming soon to popular streaming services in the U.K. You can view this as a complete list or filter results by upcoming movies and upcoming TV series.
